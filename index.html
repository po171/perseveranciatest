OUR VISION

This project is the beginning of a movement of motion. Helping the community one step at a time, accomplishing the unknown, spreading the word of virtue and the goal of becoming the biggest non profit in the United States that supports the education of all the children and teenagers of El Salvador, Central America and even Latin America.

We are currently partnered with the Liceo Juan Calvino school who is associated with the church IRCES (Iglesia Reformada Calvinista de El Salvador). The main mission of the IRCES is to build up the country's educational system and infrastructure. After once having one of the world’s highest murder rates, El Salvador has now become the country with the highest number of incarcerations due to a crackdown on gangs. IRCES has been relentlessly working to spread the word of God, kindness, hard work and family across their small community. Our current fundraising project is just a start to our long-term vision of becoming a global changing NPO.

Perseverancia is working towards changing the perspective of education, reinventing the narrative of what education looks like, allowing students to chase their passions from day one.

CURRENT MISSION

The Details:

The Juan Calvino School (Liceo Juan Calvino) is a small private school located in Soyapango, El Salvador. They only have classrooms for students from kindergarten through 6th grade. They are looking to expand their facilities to fit at least one more classroom and fill it with students ready to learn. This school is affiliated with the IRCES church also located in this small community. The end goal is to fundraise $21,000 ($16,000 for the classroom and $5,000 for the school materials).

My name is Jack Rothacker, and I am in my third year at North Carolina State University, studying Spanish & Entrepreneurship.
