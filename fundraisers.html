"El Salvador Soccer Tournament"

Event Description:

On Saturday, October 21st "Perseverancia" hosted their first fundraiser of an inter council NC State soccer tournament. Twelve teams competed in a 5v5, single elimination tournament. The event was sponsored by Celsius along with appearances from the local NPO, "El Pueblo", a dance performance from NC State's student latin organization "Sube Ritmo", and catering from "Happy Pupusas" brought by Sandra Chicas. The event raised close to $3,000 in soccer player registration, catering, t-shirt sales and sponsorships.

"Spanish Night - Redline Bar"

Event Description:

Come enjoy a night full of flavor and fun at REDLINE BAR! We invite you to our SPANISH NIGHT event, where you can immerse yourself in Spanish culture and enjoy the best music and atmosphere. The event will take place at our location at 10 Horne St #100, Raleigh, NC 27607, USA. Get ready to dance to the rhythm of Latin music, try delicious Spanish dishes and our hand selected wine menu! Tickets will enter each person into a Vintage Clothing Raffle with 3 hand selected items from Out The Box Clothing on Hillsborough street. Don't miss this opportunity to experience an authentic Spanish night at REDLINE BAR. All proceeds go to building a 7th Grade Classroom in El Salvador!
