Celsius

We aim to inspire those who want to live fit by giving them a sustained energy source that’s specially formulated to work with your body to help you reach your goals. We support those who work overtime at everything they do and never take shortcuts. We power through the extra set, we push through the last sprint, and we hustle through the workday. CELSIUS stands out against other brands because it’s made with healthier ingredients such as ginger, guarana, green tea and 7 essential vitamins. Our drinks contain no sugar, no aspartame, no high fructose corn syrup, and no artificial colors or flavors. CELSIUS is certified gluten free, kosher, and non-GMO. We’re made with nothing but the best, so you can do your best and take on any challenge that comes your way!

https://www.celsius.com
SIRE - Students for Immigrant Rights and Equality

Students for Immigrant Rights & Equality (SIRE) organizes to support the rights and equality of all kinds of immigrants. We strive to educate others about immigration, provide community service opportunities, and participate in political action to empower the immigrant community, on and off campus. Make a difference; get involved.

https://getinvolved.ncsu.edu/organization/sire
El Pueblo

We are folks who are passionate about the community that we live in. Our commitment to be for the community, by the community is at the forefront of our work. We come from diverse Latin American backgrounds with deep roots in North Carolina and we continue to learn and grow alongside our neighbors.

https://elpueblo.org/
Redline Bar

Trivia nights are every Tuesday night and Music Bingo nights are every Thursday night. Follow our Instagram page to stay tuned to events, tap takeovers, tastings, and more! Come visit us on historic Hillsborough Street across North Carolina State University. We have 24 rotating craft beer taps ! We also have multiple Wines by the glass, Champagne, Rose' & Moscato!
